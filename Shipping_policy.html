Shipping Policy

Learn about our shipping options, delivery times, and costs.

DELIVERY LOCATIONS

+

We are proud to offer nationwide delivery across Sri Lanka. Additionally, we provide international shipping to a selection of countries. Please check if your country is available during the checkout process.

  • Domestic: All provinces and districts within Sri Lanka.
  • International: Available to select countries. Please note that certain remote areas may have limited or no service.

If your location is not listed, please contact our customer support for potential custom arrangements.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

+

Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for all orders within Sri Lanka, offering you the convenience of paying at your doorstep.

  • Availability: Exclusively for domestic orders within Sri Lanka.
  • Order Limit: COD is available for orders up to a maximum value of LKR 50,000. For orders exceeding this amount, alternative payment methods (e.g., card payment, bank transfer) are required.
  • Payment: Please ensure you have the exact amount ready for the delivery agent to facilitate a smooth transaction.

For international orders, only prepaid options are available.

DELIVERY TIME

+

We strive to process and ship your orders as quickly as possible. Please note that delivery times are estimates and may vary due to external factors.

  • Order Processing: Orders are typically processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days (Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays) after successful payment confirmation.
  • Domestic Delivery (Sri Lanka):
    • Colombo & Suburbs: 2-4 business days.
    • Outstation: 3-7 business days.
  • International Delivery: Times vary significantly by destination, generally ranging from 7-21 business days. Customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes applicable in their country.

During peak seasons (e.g., holidays, sales events) or unforeseen circumstances (e.g., extreme weather, public emergencies), delivery times may be longer than usual. We appreciate your patience during such periods.

DELIVERY COST

+

Shipping costs are calculated to ensure fair pricing based on your location and order value.

  • Domestic Shipping (Sri Lanka):
    • Flat Rate: A flat rate of LKR 350 applies to all orders within Sri Lanka.
    • Free Shipping: Enjoy FREE standard delivery on all domestic orders totaling LKR 5,000 or more.
  • International Shipping:
    • Shipping costs for international orders are calculated at checkout based on the destination, weight, and dimensions of your package.
    • Please note that international customers are responsible for any customs duties, import taxes, or other fees imposed by their country's customs office. These charges are not included in the item price or shipping cost.

OTHER

+

Additional important information regarding your delivery:

  • Tracking Information: Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing a tracking number. You can use this number to monitor the status of your delivery.
  • Multiple Shipments: For orders containing multiple items, especially if they are from different warehouses or require special handling, they may be shipped separately to ensure faster delivery.
  • Signature Required: For security reasons, some high-value deliveries may require a signature upon receipt. If you are not available, the courier will leave a notification for redelivery or pickup instructions.
  • Undeliverable Packages: If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect or incomplete address provided by the customer, or if the recipient is unavailable after multiple delivery attempts, the customer will be responsible for the redelivery costs.

CHANGES AT THE TIME OF DELIVERY

+

We understand that circumstances can change. However, once an order has been dispatched, making changes to the delivery is often challenging.

  • Address Changes: Unfortunately, we cannot guarantee changes to the delivery address once an order has been shipped. Please ensure your delivery address is accurate before completing your purchase. If a change is critical, please contact the courier directly using your tracking number to see if they can accommodate your request.
  • Rescheduling Delivery: If you miss a delivery attempt, the courier will typically leave instructions for redelivery or pickup. Please contact the courier service directly to reschedule your delivery according to their policy.

For any urgent concerns regarding your delivery, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible.

CANCELLATION POLICY

+

You may cancel your order under certain conditions:

  • Before Dispatch: Orders can be cancelled for a full refund if the cancellation request is received within 2 hours of placing the order AND before the order has been processed for dispatch.
  • How to Cancel: To request a cancellation, please contact our customer service team immediately via support@zaynoonlineshop.com with your order number.
  • After Dispatch: If an order has already been dispatched, it cannot be cancelled. In such cases, the order will be treated as a return, and you will need to follow our standard Return and Exchange Policy. Please note that return shipping costs may apply.

Refunds for cancelled orders will be processed back to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.
